Gochujang Beef Bowl

Ingredients

  • 225 g (1/2 lb) 90/10 ground beef
  • Sprinkle of kosher salt
  • 50 g (~1/4 cup) gochujang paste
  • 89 g (1/2 cup) frozen peas
  • Splash of water (as needed to adjust sauce consistency)
  • 3 g (~1/2 tsp) sesame oil

Serving Components

  • Portion of steamed rice
  • Scallions, thinly sliced
  • Pickled ginger, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Sear the beef: Add the ground beef to a pan over medium heat. Add a sprinkle of salt. Let it render out some fat and sear. As it starts to brown, break it up with a wooden spoon or spatula.

  2. Sauce it up: When the beef is almost cooked through, add the gochujang and peas. Mix until well combined, and add a splash of water if the mixture is too tight. Mix or reduce until the sauce glazes the meat.

  3. Turn off the heat and finish with a drizzle of sesame oil. Taste and adjust with salt, if needed.

    Serve: Add a portion of the beef mixture over a scoop of rice. Garnish with scallions & pickled ginger. Enjoy.

Notes & Variations

This recipe can be made with any kind of ground meat. You can use any kind of diced vegetable as well.

Serve with any starch or garnish to preference.
